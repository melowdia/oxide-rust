[EN]

here is the information on the backpack script

the backpack is an inventory that persists after death so if you have items/resources/weapons they stay in place

there are 3 types of backpack

normal player backpack that has 4 places

VIP player backpack that has 5 places

admin backpack that has 10 places

of course the number is modifiable.

to open the backpack there are two possibilities either do it in the chat / backpack or by clicking on the small logo at the bottom right.

for player permissions

oxide.grant user <username> backpack.vip
oxide.grant user <username> backpack.admin
